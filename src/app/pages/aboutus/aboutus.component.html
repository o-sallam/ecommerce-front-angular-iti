A Legacy of Victorian Excellence

Founded in 1985 by antique enthusiast Margaret Thornfield, Victorian Elegance began as a small collection of carefully curated pieces discovered in English country estates. What started as a passion for preserving history has grown into one of the UK's most trusted sources for authentic Victorian furniture.

Our journey spans over three decades of dedicated sourcing, restoration, and curation. We travel across Britain and Europe, building relationships with estate agents, private collectors, and auction houses to find the finest examples of Victorian craftsmanship.
